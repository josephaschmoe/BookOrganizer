Your personal library, beautifully organized.

TomeShelf transforms your book collection into a living, searchable catalog — with AI-powered identification and research briefings to deepen your reading experience.

Features
📚
Instant Barcode Scanning

Point your camera at any book's ISBN barcode and TomeShelf instantly retrieves the title, author, cover art, and publication details — no typing required.

📷
AI Photo Identification

No barcode? No problem. Photograph the title page or copyright page and let AI identify your book automatically, even for older or obscure editions.

🔎
Book Briefings

Generate rich, AI-powered discussion guides for any book in your catalog — covering themes, historical context, key questions, and more. Spoiler controls let you read safely.

⭐
Reading Tracker

Log reading status, start and finish dates, personal notes, and star ratings. Track what you've read, what's on your shelf, and what's next.

📁
Smart Catalog

Search, sort, and filter your entire library by title, author, reading status, condition, shelf location, and more. Your collection, always at your fingertips.

📄
Portable Data

Export your catalog as JSON or CSV for safekeeping or analysis. Import from Goodreads exports or your own backups. Your data is always yours.
